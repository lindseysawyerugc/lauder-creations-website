About Me

I'm Lindsey, a 31-year-old wife, mother, former educator, and storyteller at heart.

After years in the classroom, I traded lesson plans for life at home with my growing family. Today, I create content inspired by the things I love most: intentional living, travel, the outdoors, and finding beauty in everyday moments.

When I'm not creating content, you'll find me exploring the outdoors with my family or planning our next adventure, whether it's a weekend getaway or traveling abroad. Those experiences inspire the way I create: naturally, intentionally, and with an eye for the details that make every story feel genuine.

I believe authenticity never goes out of style, and that's the foundation of every story I tell.
